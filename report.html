5. ASIL-D Physical Layer Validation (Phase 2.6)

55nm STA Data

To guarantee ASIC migration readiness for Tier-1 Edge SoCs, the RTL logic was fundamentally optimized to a 256-rule commercial target on the 55nm FPGA architecture. By integrating 100% of the patented claims—including the 128-bit Offline Store & Forward queue and Hardware Crypto Engine—we unlocked an extreme frequency boost without breaking our deterministic O(1) guarantee.

Ultra-Lightweight Footprint

Transitioning our logic mapping to utilize SSRAM on the 55nm architecture resulted in an optimal footprint of 434 LUTs, 129 REGs, and 8 SSRAM blocks (<3% logic utilization), successfully establishing an actual system Fmax of 126.035 MHz. This translates to an ASIC die area of <0.05 mm², introducing near-zero silicon cost.

O(1) Deterministic Response

By mapping the Versioned Rulepack directly to native SSRAM blocks via memory-aware synthesis, the architecture achieves a full 256-rule scan in exactly 19.04 µs. This guarantees a physical response free from OS-scheduler jitter, strictly enforcing safety boundaries for critical factory actuators.
